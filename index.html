Green Valley Medical Centre Performance Analysis

An end-to-end retail business intelligence project analyzing Q3 sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product category trends, and regional sales distribution. Using Excel dashboards and KPI-driven reporting, the analysis transformed transactional sales data into actionable insights to support Q4 business decisions.

Key Insights:
• The business generated $264K in Q3 revenue from 2,000 orders, with 4,179 items sold and an average order value of $132.
• Fashion, Home & Furniture, and Electronics contributed 63% of total Q3 revenue, emerging as the strongest product categories.
• September recorded a 30% increase in sales compared to the previous month, signaling stronger momentum heading into Q4.
• Customers aged 26–40 represented the largest customer segment, with evening periods recording the highest order activity.

Medcore Health Performance Analysis

A diagnostic financial and operational analysis tracking top-line growth against bottom-line profit compression across US furniture markets. Built an interactive Excel workspace to clean unstandardized transactional data, isolate massive margin leaks in high-volume product categories, and analyze shipping fulfillment timelines.

Key Insights:
• Despite generating $742K in revenue and increasing item volume by 11% YoY, net profitability collapsed by a brutal 57% YoY down to $18.5K.
• The profit drain is entirely driven by severe structural pricing failures in Tables and Bookcases, causing a combined loss of over -$21.1K.
• Supply chain bottlenecks show that over half of all shipments (50.3%) stack up within a lagging 4-to-5-day delivery window.
• Market demand is highly centralized in premium metropolitan economic hubs, with New York City and Los Angeles leading the nation.
